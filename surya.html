Surya

Suriya is a renowned Indian actor and producer, celebrated for his versatility in Tamil cinema. Known for his roles in films like Ghajini, Singam, and Soorarai Pottru, Suriya has earned multiple awards and a massive fan following. His dynamic performances, coupled with his philanthropic efforts, have made him one of the most respected actors in the industry. Through his work, Suriya has become an icon of hard work, dedication, and innovation in cinema.

Real Name: Saravanan Sivakumar
DOB: July 23, 1975
Native: Chennai
