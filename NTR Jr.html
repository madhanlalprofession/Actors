Young Tiger NTR Jr

NTR Jr., also known as "Young Tiger," is renowned for his powerful performances and versatility. He gained widespread recognition for his roles in RRR, Janatha Garage, and Temper. His intense acting, exceptional dance skills, and commitment to his roles make him a powerhouse in Tollywood. NTR Jr.'s fans admire his ability to seamlessly shift between intense action roles and emotional drama.

Real Name: Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr.
DOB: May 20, 1983
Native: Hyderabad
