Power Star Pawan Kalyan

Pawan Kalyan, also known as "Power Star," has an unmatched fan base due to his charismatic performances and larger-than-life screen presence. Famous for his films like Gabbar Singh and Attarintiki Daredi, Pawan is admired not only for his acting but also for his strong influence in politics and philanthropy. His action-packed roles and emotional depth make him a favorite hero in Tollywood

Real Name: Konidela Kalyan Babu.
DOB: September 2, 1971
Native: Andhra Pradesh
