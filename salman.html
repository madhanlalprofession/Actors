Salman Khan

Salman Khan, known for his action-packed roles, charismatic screen presence, and successful film career, is one of the biggest stars in Bollywood. With films like Bajrangi Bhaijaan, Sultan, and Kick, he has captivated audiences with his larger-than-life persona. Salman is also a producer, television host (Bigg Boss), and philanthropist, contributing extensively through his charitable work.

Real Name: Abdul Rashid Khan
DOB: December 27, 1965
Native: Madhya Pradesh
