Ranbir Khan

Ranbir Kapoor, one of the younger generation's biggest stars, has earned recognition for his stellar performances in films like Rockstar, Barfi!, and Sanju. Known for his intense portrayal of characters and versatility across genres, Ranbir has quickly become one of Bollywood's top actors. His family legacy and unique acting style have contributed to his success in the industry.

Real Name: Ranbir Raj Kapoor
DOB: September 28, 1982
Native: Mumbai
