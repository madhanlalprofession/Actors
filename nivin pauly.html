Nivin Pauly

Nivin Pauly has risen to fame with his endearing on-screen presence and strong performances. Known for roles in films like Premam, Action Hero Biju, and Neram, he has become a youth icon. Nivin's natural acting style and ability to connect with the audience have made him one of the most popular actors in the Malayalam film industry today.

Real Name: Nivin Pauly
