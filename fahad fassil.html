Fahad Fassil

Fahadh Faasil is known for his bold and unconventional choice of roles, which has earned him critical acclaim. With his nuanced performances in films like Maheshinte Prathikaaram, Kumbalangi Nights, and Take Off, Fahadh has established himself as one of the most talented actors in contemporary Malayalam cinema. His ability to bring authenticity to his roles has made him a standout performer.

Real Name: Fahadh Faasil
DOB: August 8, 1982
Native: Kerala
