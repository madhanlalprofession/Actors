Dulquer salmaan

Dulquer Salmaan, the son of Mammootty, has carved his own path in the Malayalam film industry with his charm, talent, and wide-ranging roles. He became a youth icon with films like Charlie and Bangalore Days, and has also worked in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi films, proving his versatility. His performances are lauded for their depth, and he continues to be one of the most sought-after stars in South Indian cinema.

Real Name: Dulquer Salmaan
DOB: May 28, 1986
Native: Kerala
