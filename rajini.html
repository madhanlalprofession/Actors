SuperStar Rajinikanth

Superstar Rajinikanth is an iconic Indian actor known for his unparalleled charisma, unique style, and exceptional performances in Tamil cinema. Born as Shivaji Rao Gaekwad on December 12, 1950, in Bengaluru, he started his career as a bus conductor before pursuing acting. His journey from humble beginnings to superstardom is inspiring. Rajinikanth's films, such as Baashha, Enthiran, and Kabali, have earned him global acclaim. Renowned for his humility and philanthropy, he continues to captivate audiences with his talent and larger-than-life persona.

Real Name: Shivaji Rao Gaekwad.
DOB: December 12, 1950.
Native: Bengalore
