Mohanlal

Mohanlal is one of the finest actors in Indian cinema, often referred to as the "Complete Actor." Known for his ability to seamlessly portray complex characters, he has become a household name with classics like Kalyanaraman, Drishyam, and Vanaprastham. With a career spanning over four decades, his contributions to Malayalam cinema are immense, and he continues to win accolades for his unmatched versatility and depth in acting.

Real Name: Mohanlal Viswanathan Nair
DOB: May 21, 1960
Native: Kerala
