Amitabh Bachchan

Amitabh Bachchan is a living legend and the "Shahenshah of Bollywood." Known for his deep voice and intense acting, he has delivered unforgettable performances in films like Sholay, Paa, and Zanjeer. His career spans over five decades, and he is revered as one of the greatest actors in Indian cinema. Apart from acting, he is a renowned television host and philanthropist.

Real Name: Amitabh Harivansh Rai Bachchan
DOB: October 11, 1942
Native: Allahabad
