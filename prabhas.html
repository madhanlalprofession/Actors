Prabhas

Prabhas is one of the most influential actors in Indian cinema, best known for his role as Baahubali in the Baahubali franchise. His intense performances, strong screen presence, and commitment to challenging roles have made him a pan-Indian superstar. His dedication to fitness and his role in high-budget films like Saaho further cemented his status as a beloved action hero

Real Name: Uppalapati Venkata Krishnam Raju Prabhas
DOB: October 23, 1979
Native: Chennai
