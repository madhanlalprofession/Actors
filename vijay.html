Thalapathy vijay

Thalapathy Vijay, a celebrated Indian actor, is renowned for his impactful performances in Tamil cinema. Known for his charismatic screen presence, he has delivered blockbuster hits like Ghilli, Thuppakki, and Master. With his versatility in action, romance, and comedy, Vijay has earned immense admiration and a massive global fanbase. Beyond acting, he is a skilled playback singer and a prominent figure in South Indian cinema, inspiring millions with his dedication and style.

Real Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar
DOB: June 22, 1974
Native: Tamil Nadu
