Ulaga Nayagan Kamala Hasan

Kamal Haasan is a legendary Indian actor, filmmaker, and politician celebrated for his versatility and groundbreaking contributions to cinema. Born on November 7, 1954, in Paramakudi, Tamil Nadu, he began his acting career as a child artist and evolved into a powerhouse performer. Known for iconic films like Nayakan, Indian, and Vikram, he has won numerous awards, including multiple National Film Awards. Kamal is also a skilled writer, singer, and director, continually pushing creative boundaries and leaving a profound impact on Indian cinema.

Real Name: Parthasarathy Srinivasan
DOB: November 7, 1954
Native: Tamil Nadu
