Thala ajith

Ajith Kumar, popularly known as Thala Ajith, is a celebrated actor in Tamil cinema. Renowned for his versatile roles in action and drama, he has delivered numerous successful films like Mankatha, Veeram, and Viswasam. Apart from acting, Ajith is an avid motorsport enthusiast and has participated in car racing events. His humility, dedication, and distinct screen presence have earned him a massive fan following, making him one of the most respected stars in the industry.

Real Name: Ajith Kumar
DOB: May 1, 1971
Native: Hyderabad
