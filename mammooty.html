Mammooty

Mammootty is one of the most celebrated actors in Malayalam cinema, known for his intense and charismatic performances. With iconic roles in films like New Year, Bharathchandran I.P.S., and Pazhassi Raja, he has earned numerous accolades, including National Film Awards. Mammootty's ability to adapt to diverse roles, from action-packed heroes to deep emotional characters, has made him a beloved figure in South Indian cinema.

Real Name: Muhammad Kutty Ismail Paniparambil
DOB: September 7, 1951
Native: Kerala
