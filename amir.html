Amir Khan

Aamir Khan is known for his versatility and willingness to take on unconventional roles. Films like Lagaan, PK, and Dangal have earned him national and international acclaim. Aamir’s ability to adapt to different genres, his commitment to his roles, and his involvement in film production have made him one of Bollywood’s most respected figures.

Real Name: Aamir Hussain Khan
DOB: March 14, 1965
Native: Mumbai
