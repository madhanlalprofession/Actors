Shah Rukh Khan

Shah Rukh Khan, known as the "King of Bollywood," has built an unparalleled career with iconic films like Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, My Name Is Khan, and Chennai Express. His ability to portray romance, drama, and action, coupled with his immense charm, has earned him a massive fan base worldwide. He is not just an actor but a global icon, known for his contributions to the film industry and philanthropy.

Real Name: Shah Rukh Khan
DOB: November 2, 1965
Native: New Delhi
