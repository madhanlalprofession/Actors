Prince of Tollywood Mahesh Babu

Mahesh Babu, often called the "Prince of Tollywood," is known for his charm, acting versatility, and screen presence. With iconic roles in films like Maharshi, Srimanthudu, and Sarileru Neekevvaru, he has earned several accolades and a massive fan following. His blend of action, romance, and emotional depth has made him a favorite among audiences.

Real Name: Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu
DOB: August 9, 1975
Native: Chennai
